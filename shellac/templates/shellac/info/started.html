Getting Started

Navigation Bar

The navigation bar is affixed atop and collapses into the icon (top, right) in mobile browsers.

  • People: List users by date joined
  • Relations: Follow or Unfollow people
  • Manages your playlist feed
  • Clips: View a list of your clips. Update / Delete
  • Upload: Upload a new Clip
  • Settings: Dropdown menu
  • My Account
  • Sign Out
  • Site information

Audio Player

Clicking the    navigates to the player page. The clips shown are those from people that you have chosen to 'follow'. The audio player is affixed at the bottom.

  • Play the current item in the queue
  • Play the previous item in the queue
  • Play the next item in the queue
  • Save playlist and clip positions
  • Adjust volume (disabled mobile)
  • Toggle playlist menu
